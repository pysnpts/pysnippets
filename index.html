About PySnippets

PySnippets provides a seamless experience to quickly run and share simple Python code in the browser, thanks to PyScript.

PySnippets are lightweight and fast, making them ideal for quick experiments and learning. We support both MicroPython and Pyodide runtimes. Other settings refine your snippet's Python environment. The PyScript docs are your friend, as are the friendly folk in the PyScript Discord community.

Best of all, share your snippets with others through the simplicity of a link.

Whether you're a beginner or an experienced developer, PySnippets offers a straightforward way to write and share Python code without the overhead of complex configuration.

(P.S. Did we mention it's also open-source?)

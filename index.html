About PySnippets

Quickly write, run and share Python code in the browser, thanks to PyScript.

PySnippets are lightweight and fast, making them ideal for quick experiments and learning. We support both MicroPython and Pyodide runtimes. Define your snippet's Python environment with other settings. The PyScript docs are your friend, as are the friendly folk in the PyScript Discord community.

Best of all, share your creations with others through the simplicity of a link.

Whether you're a beginner or an experienced developer, write and share Python code without the pain of complex configuration.

(P.S. It's also open-source!)

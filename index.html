About PySnippets

PySnippets combines the power of Python with the simplicity of online code snippets. Put simply, we provide a seamless experience to quickly run and share simple Python code in the browser, thanks to PyScript.

PySnippets are lightweight and fast, making them ideal for quick experiments and learning. We support both MicroPython and Pyodide runtimes: choose which one to use based on your Pythonic needs. Other settings refine your code's Python environment. The PyScript docs are your friend, as are the friendly folk in the PyScript Discord community.

Best of all, share your snippets with others through the simplicity of a link.

Whether you're a beginner or an experienced developer, PySnippets offers a straightforward way to write and share Python code without the overhead of complex setups.

(P.S. Did we mention it's also open-source?)
